## REASONS FOR GRANTING THE WRIT

### I. The Jurisdictional Shell Game as a Due Process Violation
As held in *Hohri*, the manipulation of jurisdictional statutes to deprive a litigant of a forum is a violation of fundamental fairness. The use of § 1631 to bypass the Court of Federal Claims and facilitate the seizure of real and intellectual property represents a structural collapse of constitutional protections.

### II. Public Trust IP and the Takings Clause
The Petitioner’s genomic IP is "Public Trust IP" developed to fulfill federal accessibility mandates. By seizing the physical residence where this technology was managed and simultaneously barring its use in court, the government has effected a total regulatory taking under *Lucas*, rendering Petitioner's assets "economically idle."
