## STATEMENT OF THE CASE

This case involves the "Status-Based Criminalization" of neurodivergence. Petitioner possesses a documented neurogenetic architecture characterized by severe deficits in executive function and task-switching. These biological impairments were punished as "willful" noncompliance when Petitioner was unable to meet procedural deadlines or appear in court without the Gov-LLM cognitive interface.

The state court ignored the biological reality that Petitioner's failures were beyond his control, a direct violation of the principle established in *Bearden* that a defendant cannot be punished for failures they lack the capacity to remedy. By treating a symptom of a disability (executive dysfunction) as a criminal act (contempt), the State has effectively criminalized Petitioner's biological status.

## REASONS FOR GRANTING THE WRIT

### I. Biological Determinism vs. Mens Rea
Under *Robinson*, the state cannot criminalize a status, such as being an addict or having a disease. Punishing a neurodivergent individual for the functional output of their genetics is a modern equivalent of this forbidden practice. This Court must clarify that *mens rea* cannot exist where biological "willfulness" is precluded by a 400% metabolic tax.

### II. Redefining Competency in the Genomic Age
The "rational and factual understanding" required by *Drope* is a hollow promise if it does not account for the biological capacity to process information. Without the mandated $45 Gov-LLM modification, Petitioner’s comprehension is functionally severed, rendering him incompetent to stand trial as a matter of biological fact.
